Sudoku365 Classic Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-08

Data Collection

Sudoku365 Classic does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to me or third parties.

Contact

If you contact me directly (e.g., via email), i may receive your email address and any information you include. This will only be used to respond to your inquiry.

GitHub Issues

For bug reports and feature requests, users may choose to open an issue on my public GitHub repository. Please note that information you provide in a GitHub issue (including your username and any content you submit) is public and visible to others. I do not control GitHub's platform and recommend reviewing GitHub's Privacy Policy.

Questions

If you have questions about this policy, contact me at devdellsstudio@gmail.com.
